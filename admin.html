📊 How to Handle Common Situations
REVISIONS
Client wants changes
Get specific feedback in writing. Pass to CEO with clear notes. Set 48hr expectation for turnaround — then follow up with the client once done.
UNHAPPY
Client is frustrated
Acknowledge and apologise first. Escalate to CEO immediately. Never argue. Aim to resolve within 24 hours — follow up personally once resolved.
GONE QUIET
Client not responding
Two attempts over 5 days. If still silent, pause the project and document it. Not your fault — but log it so the CEO knows the status.
UPSELL
Retainer conversation
Plant the seed at project end. Follow up exactly 2 weeks later. If they say not yet — diary a reminder for 6 weeks and try again. Timing matters.
